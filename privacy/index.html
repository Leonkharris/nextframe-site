Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

This policy explains what information Next-Frame.Agency ("we", "us") collects when you contact us or request a free audit, how we use it, and the choices you have. We handle personal information in line with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173).

What we collect

  • Information you send us — when you submit our intake or free-audit form, we collect details such as your name, email address, business name, and the page, offer, or content you ask us to review.
  • Basic usage data — like most websites, our hosting provider may log standard technical data (e.g. browser type and approximate region) for security and reliability. This site is served as static pages and does not run our own advertising or cross-site tracking.

How we use it

  • To prepare and send your free audit and a custom quote.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide our services if you become a client.
  • To improve our own content and offers.

What we do not do

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with third parties except trusted service providers that help us operate (for example, email or hosting), and only as needed to deliver what you asked for.

Retention

We keep the information you send only as long as needed to respond to you and, if you become a client, to provide our services and meet legal or accounting requirements. You can ask us to delete it at any time.

Your rights

Under the Data Privacy Act, you have the right to access, correct, object to the processing of, and request deletion of your personal information. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the form below and we will respond within a reasonable time.

Contact

For any privacy request or question, reach us through our contact / free-audit form and note that your message is about privacy.
