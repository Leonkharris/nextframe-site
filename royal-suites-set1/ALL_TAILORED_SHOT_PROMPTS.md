```text
Create a high resolution 3D isometric floor plan render based EXACTLY on the provided 2D architectural floor plan image.

- Wardrobes only where shown in plan.

Render Settings:
- 9:16 vertical output, 1080x1920 or taller vertical equivalent.
- The rendered apartment must fit cleanly inside a vertical frame.
- Isometric top angle view, dollhouse view.
- Soft daylight lighting.
- Ambient occlusion shadows.
- Neutral background.
- Isolated cut-out apartment, no terrain.
- Photorealistic materials.
