About Next-Frame.Agency

We're a Makati-based AI marketing agency helping Philippine small businesses turn views into leads — using AI for production volume, with a human owning strategy and approving everything.

What we believe

Most small businesses don't lose to competitors because their offer is worse — they lose because they can't produce and respond fast enough. Posting consistently, replying to inquiries in seconds, and keeping ads fresh are production problems. AI removes that bottleneck, so a small team can show up like a much bigger one.

How we work

  • AI for volume, humans for judgment — AI drafts content, reels, and first-touch replies; a person directs strategy, brand voice, and final approval.
  • Nothing posts without your sign-off — you stay in control of everything that goes out.
  • Measured on leads, not likes — we focus on what actually drives inquiries and sales.

Who we help

Philippine small and medium businesses — real estate agents and developers, hospitality and venues, and local service businesses — that want consistent, practical marketing without a full in-house team or traditional-agency rates. How we got here

Next-Frame grew out of 20+ years of hands-on production work across hospitality, nightlife, entertainment, gaming, and real estate — and a simple observation: the businesses that win online are rarely the ones with the single best ad. They are the ones that show up consistently and respond fast. Generative AI finally made that level of consistency affordable for small businesses, not just brands with big retainers. We built Next-Frame to be the bridge between that technology and a business owner’s real goals — pairing production craft earned over two decades with modern AI workflows, so the output is fast and high-volume without losing the human judgment that keeps it on-brand.
