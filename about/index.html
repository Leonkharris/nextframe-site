About Next-Frame.Agency

We're a Makati-based AI marketing agency helping Philippine small businesses turn views into leads — using AI for production volume, with a human owning strategy and approving everything.

What we believe

Most small businesses don't lose to competitors because their offer is worse — they lose because they can't produce and respond fast enough. Posting consistently, replying to inquiries in seconds, and keeping ads fresh are production problems. AI removes that bottleneck, so a small team can show up like a much bigger one.

How we work

  • AI for volume, humans for judgment — AI drafts content, reels, and first-touch replies; a person directs strategy, brand voice, and final approval.
  • Nothing posts without your sign-off — you stay in control of everything that goes out.
  • Measured on leads, not likes — we focus on what actually drives inquiries and sales.

Who we help

Philippine small and medium businesses — real estate agents and developers, hospitality and venues, and local service businesses — that want consistent, practical marketing without a full in-house team or traditional-agency rates.
