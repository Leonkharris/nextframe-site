# NEXT-FRAME.AGENCY MASTER OPERATING MANUAL

## 1. MISSION STATEMENT
To be the 'Human Bridge' between business intuition and AI execution. We solve revenue problems by automating the production of high-converting marketing assets.

## 2. THE CLIENT INTAKE PIPELINE
Every client (including our first client: NEXT-FRAME) follows this 3-Phase logic:

### PHASE A: DISCOVERY & DNA (The Investigator)
- Task 1: DNA Probe: Scrape client site/socials to define 'Brand Voice'.
- Task 2: Market Gap Audit: Identify what competitors (Top 3) are failing to do.
- Task 3: Marketing Blueprint: Plan the target audience and hook strategy.

### PHASE B: PRODUCTION (The Architect & Asset Forge)
- Step 1: Storyboard & Strategy: Detailed scene-by-scene marketing narrative.
- Step 2: Asset Generation: Create keyframes, model assets, brand stills, and prompts.
- Step 3: Assembly: AI video generation (Kling/Veo) and final editing.

### PHASE C: DISTRIBUTION (The Assembler)
- Automated Deployment: Post and manage ads across Facebook, TikTok, and YouTube.
- Data Feedback: Track performance and refine the DNA for the next loop.

## 3. PRICING TIERS

- Tier 2 (Mid-Size): ₱30,000 (Full campaign).
- Tier 3 (Enterprise): ₱60,000 - ₱80,000 (Retainer + Full Automation Engine).
