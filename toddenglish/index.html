About the Chef

A Culinary Legacy Spanning Three Decades

Todd English is one of America's most celebrated chefs and hospitality entrepreneurs. A James Beard Award winner and member of the James Beard Foundation's "Who's Who in Food & Beverage in America," he has spent more than three decades creating award-winning restaurants, luxury hotel dining experiences, food halls, and hospitality brands around the world.


A graduate of the Culinary Institute of America who honed his craft under Jean-Jacques Rachou at La Côte Basque and through apprenticeships across Italy, English burst onto the national scene when he opened his flagship restaurant Olives in Charlestown, Massachusetts, in 1989. He went on to create more than 40 restaurant concepts across 12 countries.


Today, Todd English continues to develop restaurants, hotels, resorts, food halls, airports, private clubs, and luxury residential dining concepts — including his newest concept, Proper English, at the Bentley Residences in Miami.

Featured Concepts

Signature Dining Experiences

Flagship Culinary Art

bluezoo

Located inside the Walt Disney World Dolphin Hotel, bluezoo transforms coastal cuisine into fine art. Featured here is the signature pan-seared red snapper glazed with citrus reduction, served with fresh dill, sea salt, and citrus wedges on dark textured stoneware. The dish embodies the restaurant's commitment to premium, coastal dining excellence.

International

Jade by Todd English

Set in the heart of Bali's vibrant Berawa district, Jade by Todd English is a Mediterranean-Asian fusion restaurant and nightlife destination. Lush tropical gardens, open-air dining pavilions, and a celebrated cocktail bar make Jade one of Canggu's most talked-about venues — blending world-class cuisine with Bali's legendary hospitality culture.

Entertainment Dining

The Beast

Inside Las Vegas's immersive entertainment complex AREA15, The Beast is a steampunk-inspired gastropub unlike any other. Oversized copper machinery, industrial décor, and a theatrical dining format create an experience that blurs the line between restaurant and attraction. Bold, indulgent comfort food meets one-of-a-kind entertainment dining.
