How Much Does Social Media Management Cost in the Philippines? (2026 Guide)

Short answer: most Philippine businesses pay a monthly retainer between ₱15,000 and ₱150,000+, depending on scope. Here's what actually drives that number — and why AI-powered management lands at the lower end.

Typical 2026 monthly retainers in the Philippines

General market ranges, not a fixed price list — your cost depends on the scope below.

TierTypical / monthScope
Starter₱15k–35k1 platform, 8–12 posts, scheduling + basic replies
Growth₱35k–80k2–3 platforms, content + captions, community management, monthly report
Full-service₱80k–150k+Multi-platform, Reels/video production, paid ads + creatives, lead follow-up

What drives the cost

  • Number of platforms — Facebook only vs Facebook + Instagram + TikTok + LinkedIn.
  • Posting frequency — a few posts a week vs daily content.
  • Content production — reusing your photos vs producing original graphics, Reels, and short video.
  • Paid ads — organic posting only vs managed ad campaigns (ad spend is separate).
  • Strategy & reporting depth — post-and-pray vs measured against leads and sales.

Traditional agency vs AI-powered management

A traditional agency prices in the human hours of writers, designers, and editors — so a full content month is expensive. An AI-powered approach automates the drafting, design, and repurposing, so the same output volume costs less. For a small business, that's the difference between agency-level consistency at a small-business budget.

Frequently asked questions

How much does social media management cost in the Philippines?

In 2026, monthly retainers commonly range from ~₱15,000 for a single-platform starter scope to ₱150,000+ for full multi-platform management with ads and video. The driver is scope, not headcount — AI-powered production lands at the lower end of the range.

What's included in a retainer?

Strategy, a content calendar, post and caption creation, scheduling, community management (comments + DMs), and a performance report. Higher tiers add Reels/video, paid-ad creatives and management, and lead follow-up.

Is AI-powered management cheaper than a traditional agency?

Usually yes — automating content drafting, design, and repurposing removes the hours a traditional agency bills for, so the same output costs less.
