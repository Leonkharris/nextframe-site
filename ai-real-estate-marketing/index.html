What AI handles for a PH real estate agent

  • Listings → Reels: each property becomes a short-form video + a set of social posts, automatically.
  • Instant inquiry replies: auto-drafted responses to Facebook and Viber buyer messages in seconds — never lose a lead to a slow reply again.
  • Ad creatives: scroll-stopping creatives ready to boost, so your best listings actually get seen.
  • Consistency: agency-level output volume without hiring an agency-sized team.

Why it works for agents specifically

Real estate is a speed-and-volume game: the agent who replies first and shows the most listings wins. Both are production problems, and production is exactly what AI removes the bottleneck on. You stay the face and the closer; the system handles the content and the first-touch.

Next-Frame builds and runs that system for you — including a listing-intake workflow that turns raw property details into ready-to-post marketing.

What you get with every listing

Each property you give us becomes a complete content set, not a single post. From one set of photos and the listing details, the system produces a short-form Reel built for Facebook and TikTok, a carousel for the feed, a written caption with the right hooks, and an ad creative ready to boost. The same listing also gets a set of auto-reply templates, so the first message from a buyer is answered in seconds instead of hours.

The point is leverage: one property, a week’s worth of content, produced in a fraction of the time it takes to brief a videographer, wait for edits, and write the captions yourself. You stay the agent and the closer; the production runs in the background.

How a listing becomes content

  • You submit the listing — photos, price, location, and the key selling points, through a simple intake. No scripts or editing software on your end.
  • The system produces the pack — Reel, posts, caption, and ad creative are generated around your brand and the property.
  • You approve — review the pack, tweak anything, and sign off. Nothing publishes without you.
  • It goes out and replies for you — content is scheduled and inquiry replies are auto-drafted, so a buyer messaging at 11pm still gets an instant first response.

Built for how Philippine buyers actually search

In the Philippines, property discovery and first contact happen on Facebook, Messenger, Viber, and increasingly TikTok — not only on listing portals. Buyers scroll, save the Reels that catch them, and message the agent who feels responsive and real. Two things decide who wins that buyer: how much good content you put in front of them, and how fast you reply when they reach out. Both are production problems, and production is the part AI removes the bottleneck on. Filipinos also spend among the most time on social media of any country — roughly 3.5 hours per day on average (DataReportal, Digital 2025) — so for a property, the feed is where most buyers first meet your listing.

A traditional setup forces a choice: hire a videographer and editor (slow and expensive), or do it yourself between viewings (inconsistent). An AI content system gives you agency-level output without either trade-off — you direct, it produces, and you keep full control of what goes live.
